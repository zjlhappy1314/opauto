Our Core Solutions

At Openex, we deliver cutting-edge AI-powered vision inspection and industrial automation solutions that enable manufacturers to achieve zero-defect quality control and high throughput across a wide range of industries.

AI Vision Inspection Machines

Our AI vision inspection systems provide automated, high-speed, non-contact inspection of fasteners, automotive parts, bearings, lithium batteries, semiconductor components, and more. Integrated deep learning algorithms detect complex and minute defects to ensure every product meets the highest quality standards.

Industrial Automation Equipment

From automatic packaging to robotic assembly lines, our integrated automation solutions maximize production efficiency and product quality. We provide vision systems and complete machines to customers requiring industrial automation solutions to provide accurate inspection, control and feedback.

AR Remote Assistance

Our augmented reality (AR) remote solutions empower your teams with real-time remote troubleshooting and inspection capabilities. This innovative technology enhances operational efficiency across manufacturing, pharmaceutical, automotive, agriculture, and healthcare sectors.

Disposable Hygiene Production Lines

Through our subsidiary Shuaixin Tech, we offer custom converting machines and complete production line solutions for disposable hygienic products. With over 20 years of industry expertise, we ensure high reliability, efficiency, and cost-effectiveness in your hygiene manufacturing processes.
