About Openex Automation

Since our inception, Openex has been dedicated to delivering high-speed, reliable AI vision inspection machines and industrial automation solutions. Our mission is to help manufacturers eliminate manual inspections and ensure 100% quality control—achieving better traceability, accuracy, and productivity.

With over 60 engineers, including 10 master's degree holders, we serve customers across industries such as automotive, medical, electronics, packaging, and energy.

Our Products and Solutions

High-precision inspection technologies that inspect concealed defects fast and reliably are prerequisites for flawless, 100% reliable products. Our wide range of state-of-the-art inspection systems offers the right solution for every inspection task. Explore our delivered products demonstrating our expertise in AI vision inspection and industrial automation across diverse industries worldwide.

Automated Optical Sorting Machine

High-speed 360° vision inspection for fasteners, bearings, auto parts, etc.
Detects dimensional errors, surface cracks, color mix-ups, and symbol recognition at up to 1,200 pcs/min.

Automated Inspection and Palletizing Solution

A turnkey line for post-production handling of precision-machined parts: inline gauging, vision QC, robotic pick-and-place, and smart palletizing with barcode traceability.

Automatic Assembling and Inspection Machine

Dedicated workstation for exhaust pipe joints with servo-driven pressing, leak testing, torque monitoring, and in-line camera inspection for 100% assurance.

Automatic Grinding and Inspection Machine

For GIS insulated components, combines precision grinding, adaptive force control, and multi-camera inspection to ensure tight tolerances and defect-free edges.
