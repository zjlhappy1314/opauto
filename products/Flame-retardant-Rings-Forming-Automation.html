  • Material Feeding - Stainless steel strips with pre-manufactured folds are automatically fed into the forming station.

  • Inline Inspection - High-resolution vision inspection verifies dimensions, surface quality, and fold integrity.
  • Data Logging - All inspection results are recorded in the system database for traceability.
  • Output Handling - Good parts are transferred to the next stage or packaging; defective rings are automatically rejected.
