Overview

Nuts are widely used in automotive, aerospace, electronics, construction, and heavy machinery. As critical fastening components, even the smallest defect can compromise safety and performance. Manual inspection is no longer practical for high-volume production.

Openex nuts inspection machines provide manufacturers with:

  • Complete quality assurance by inspecting every piece
  • Dimensional and surface defect detection with micron-level precision
  • High-speed throughput without slowing down production
  • Automated sorting and data recording for traceability

Inspection Capabilities

Our nuts inspection systems can detect:

  • Dimensional Measurements: Inner diameter, outer diameter, height, thread pitch
  • Thread Integrity: Cross-threading, incomplete threads, pitch errors
  • Surface Defects: Cracks, scratches, burrs, dents, deformation, rust
  • Coating & Color: Detect plating defects, color variations, and surface contamination
  • Stamping & Mark Recognition: Verify embossed symbols, logos, or lot numbers
  • Sorting: Automatic OK/NG separation with pneumatic ejectors
