Overview

Openex Washers Inspection Machine is designed to ensure 100% quality control for both metal and plastic washers. With advanced AI vision inspection technology, the system can detect dimensional deviations, cracks, burrs, surface scratches, color variations, and flatness errors at high speed. The system integrates a vibration bowl for alignment, a high-precision rotating glass disk for positioning, and multi-angle cameras for complete inspection coverage. Pneumatic ejectors automatically separate good and defective washers, ensuring zero-defect delivery.

Inspection Capabilities

Our advanced optical inspection systems are designed to ensure 100% quality control of washers, guaranteeing dimensional accuracy and surface integrity before they reach your customers. Using high-speed cameras, precision lenses, and AI-driven image processing, our equipment detects even the smallest defects and deviations.

Key Inspection Parameters for Washers

  • Outer Diameter (OD): Precise measurement to ensure washers fit perfectly in assemblies.
  • Inner Diameter (ID): Accurate detection to avoid issues with shafts, bolts, or screws.
  • Thickness: Micron-level thickness measurement for uniformity and compliance with standards.
  • Flatness / Warpage: Ensures washers remain flat without bending or deformation.
  • Surface Defects: Identification of scratches, cracks, dents, corrosion, or contamination.
  • Edge Defects: Detection of burrs, chips, or incomplete cuts on inner and outer edges.
  • Coating & Color: Verifies plating quality, uniformity of coating, and correct washer color.
  • Material Mix-up Prevention: Differentiates washers by material type, finish, and size.
