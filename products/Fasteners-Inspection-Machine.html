Product Overview

Fasteners are safety-critical components. Even the smallest dimensional inaccuracy or surface defect can cause serious failure in automotive, aerospace, energy, or industrial applications.
Openex fastener inspection systems automate the inspection process using high-resolution cameras, intelligent image processing software, and high-speed part handling. Each machine is optimized to deliver accurate, fast, and repeatable inspection of fasteners in various shapes, sizes, and materials.

    • How Is The Fastener Inspection Machine Works?

  • 1, Part Feeding: Vibratory bowl or conveyor aligns fasteners in the correct orientation.
  • 2, Image Capture: High-speed industrial cameras capture images from multiple angles.
  • 3, Processing: Vision software analyzes images for dimensions, defects, and symbols.
  • 4, Decision Making: Parts are judged as OK or NG based on customizable tolerances.
  • 5, Sorting:Pneumatic actuators or diverters sort parts into bins accordingly.
