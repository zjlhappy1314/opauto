Our systems leverage advanced industrial cameras, deep learning algorithms, and non-contact inspection technologies to ensure that every component is thoroughly examined ‚Äî from leadframe alignment and solder joint integrity to micro-cracks and surface anomalies invisible to the naked eye.

What Can Our Machines Inspect?

Our semiconductor inspection systems are flexible and adaptable to a wide range of components and applications:

  • Inspected Components Include:
    • - IC chips (QFP, BGA, LGA, CSP, etc.)
    • - PCBs (Populated/Unpopulated)
    • - Wafers
    • - Connectors
    • - Solder joints
    • - SMD components
    • - Optical sensors
    • - Leadframes
  • Detectable Defects:
    • - Pits
    • - Dents
    • - Voids
    • - Crumbling
    • - Missing components
    • - Delamination
    • - Short circuits & open leads
    • - Warping and deformation
    • - Micro-cracks and chipping
    • - Oxidation or discoloration
    • - Scratch & burrs on surface
    • - 2D/3D dimension deviation
