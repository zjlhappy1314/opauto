Product Overview

Our production line integrates composite forming, precision die-cutting, AI-powered inspection, and automatic packaging into one seamless process. With a maximum speed of 350 pcs/min, it ensures your products meet the highest hygiene, safety, and comfort standards — all while optimizing space and reducing operating costs.
Designed for:

  • Large-scale manufacturers requiring maximum output
  • Cleanroom and high-hygiene environments
  • Operations prioritizing quality, efficiency, and automation

Technical Specifications

  • - Max Production Speed : 350 pcs/min
  • - Product Types : Diapers, sanitary napkins, nursing pads, masks, other non-woven hygiene products
  • - Material Compatibility : Non-woven fabrics, polymer particles, elastic materials
  • - Control System : Multi-axis synchronized control with constant tension management
  • - Cutting Accuracy : High-precision die-cutting system
  • - Inspection : Integrated AI vision inspection for real-time defect detection
  • - Environment : Suitable for cleanroom production

Application

For the high-speed production of various hygiene products including:

  • Baby Diapers
  • Sanitary Napkins
  • Nursing Pads
  • Face Masks
  • Other non-woven fabric-based hygiene and protective items

Key Technical Features

  • Constant Tension Control Production - Ensures material stability and precision alignment at high speeds.
  • Synchronized Multi-Shaft Control - Coordinates multiple high-speed rotating shafts for flawless operation.
  • Precision Die-Cutting Control - Guarantees accurate shaping of each product with minimal material waste.
  • Polymer Particle Layering - Accurately adds absorbent polymer particles between multiple non-woven layers for optimal product performance.
  • High-Speed Output - Produces up to 350 pieces per minute with consistent quality.
  • Space-Efficient Design - Compact footprint ideal for maximizing production space.
  • Cleanroom Compatible - Suitable for hygiene-critical manufacturing environments.
  • Profit-Driven Productivity - Operates “like money printing” by maximizing throughput while maintaining quality.
