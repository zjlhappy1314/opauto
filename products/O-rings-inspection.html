Overview

O-rings play a critical role in ensuring fluid-tight sealing in automotive, aerospace, medical, and industrial equipment. Even the smallest defect can lead to leakage, system failure, or costly warranty claims. Traditional manual inspection is unreliable and inefficient for large-scale production.

Openex O-Ring Inspection Machines integrate machine vision, AI defect recognition, and robotic sorting to deliver reliable, high-throughput, non-contact inspection solutions..

Inspection Capabilities

Our systems are designed to detect a wide range of defects in rubber, plastic, and elastomer O-rings:

  • Dimensional Accuracy
    Inner diameter (ID), outer diameter (OD), cross-sectional thickness, concentricity
  • Surface Defects
    Cracks, cuts, scratches, notches, pores, bubbles
  • Shape Defects
    Ovality, deformation, twisting, incomplete molding
  • Material Defects
    Flash, excess material, missing material, inclusions
  • Color Consistency & Symbol Recognition (for coded O-rings)
