Why Rivet Inspection Matters

Rivets are small but critical fasteners widely used in automotive structures, aircraft components, electronic devices, and consumer products. A single defective rivet can cause assembly failures, safety hazards, and costly recalls. Traditional manual inspection cannot keep up with large-scale production and often misses subtle defects.

Openex Rivet Inspection Machines provide non-contact, automated inspection that ensures every rivet meets stringent quality requirements — without slowing down production.

Inspection Capabilities

Our rivet inspection systems combine industrial-grade cameras, precision lighting, and AI-powered analysis software to detect:

Dimensional Accuracy
  • - Head diameter, shank diameter, shank length, concentricity, and tolerances.
Surface Defects
  • - Cracks, notches, scratches, burrs, dents, or surface contamination.
Color & Coating Verification
  • - Ensure correct plating, coatings, and surface finishes for functional and aesthetic requirements.
Symbol & Mark Recognition
  • - Detect stamped marks, logos, or codes for traceability and compliance.
Deformation & Assembly Issues
  • - Bent rivets, incorrect shapes, incomplete forming, or misaligned heads.

    • How It Works ?

  • Feeding System
    Vibratory bowl aligns and feeds rivets onto the rotating disk.
  • Image Capture
    Multiple cameras capture rivet images from all sides under controlled lighting.
  • AI Analysis
    Proprietary deep-learning algorithms analyze images in real time.
  • Sorting
    Pneumatic ejectors separate defective rivets (NG) from good rivets (OK).
  • Data & Traceability
    Inspection results stored in the system for quality records and process improvement.
