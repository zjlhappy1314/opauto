Overview

Studs are critical fasteners used in automotive, construction, engineering, and energy industries. Any defects in size, thread, or surface integrity can cause safety issues, assembly failures, or costly recalls. Openex studs inspection machines combine high-speed optical imaging, AI-driven defect detection, and precision measurement to deliver reliable inspection results for every single piece, not just samples.

Our system integrates seamlessly into production lines, helping manufacturers achieve:

  • 🔍 Full part traceability
  • ⚡ High throughput up to thousands of studs per minute
  • 🎯 Consistent, repeatable inspection accuracy

    • Inspection Capabilities

    Our studs inspection machines are designed to identify and measure critical quality features:

    Dimensional Measurement
    • Shank length
    • Shank diameter
    • Head diameter
    • Thread length & pitch
    • Concentricity
    Surface Defects Detection
    • Cracks, notches, dents
    • Scratches and surface contamination
    • Burrs and incomplete machining
    • Color consistency
    Thread Quality & Completeness
    • Missing threads
    • Damaged or deformed threads
    • Thread angle accuracy
    Marking & Symbol Recognition
    • Character/logo presence check
    • Correctness and alignment
