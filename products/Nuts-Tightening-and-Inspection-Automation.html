Product Overview

The Nuts Tightening and Inspection Automation System from Openex is a high-precision, fully automated solution designed for the assembly and quality control of battery case top plates.
It performs automatic tightening of nuts while simultaneously checking torque accuracy, rotation parameters, and fastening quality, ensuring consistency, reliability, and traceability in large-scale battery manufacturing.
Built on our advanced servo-controlled tightening technology and integrated vision/torque inspection systems, this machine delivers unmatched precision for electric vehicle batteries, energy storage modules, and other battery pack applications.

Key Features

Multi-Product Compatibility

  • Handles different nut sizes, battery case designs, and product types in one machine without extensive changeover time.
    • Full Torque & Speed Control
  • Adjustable torque, rotation speed, waiting time, and rotation direction to meet various process requirements.
    • High Precision Servo Tightening
  • Torque Error: ≤ ±3%
  • Speed Error: ≤ ±3%
  • Torque Control Accuracy: max 0.1 N·m
  • Torque Range: 0.5 N·m to 5 N·m

    • Traceability & Data Logging
  • All inspection results are recorded for quality tracking and production reporting.
