Product Overview

Our Powder Metallurgy Parts Inspection System is designed for non-contact, inline inspection of sintered and compacted metal parts, including gears, bushings, brackets, filters, and structural components. Using a combination of high-resolution cameras, custom lighting setups, and deep-learning-based defect classification, our system detects visual anomalies with sub-pixel accuracy.
Whether you're inspecting 50 or 5,000 parts per hour, Openex delivers consistent, real-time, reliable inspection.

Key Inspection Capabilities

Dimensional Measurement

  • Outer and inner diameter
  • Height and thickness
  • Flatness and roundness
  • Surface area

Surface Defect Detection

  • Cracks, pores, pits, chips
  • Burn marks, discoloration, stains
  • Sintering flaws and abnormal texture
  • Edge burrs and broken corners

Shape Integrity

  • Contour analysis of complex geometries
  • Detection of warping or deformation
  • Hole offset or misalignment

System Features

  • High-Speed Inspection: Up to 300 parts/min depending on part size and complexity
  • 360° View: Multi-camera and rotating mechanism for full part surface coverage
  • Real-Time Sorting: Pneumatic or mechanical rejectors for OK/NG separation
  • Traceability: Image and result archiving for every part inspected
  • Compact Design: Small footprint, easy integration into existing production lines
  • Touchscreen Interface: Intuitive UI for inspection setup, tuning, and monitoring
