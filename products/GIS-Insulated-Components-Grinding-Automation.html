Product Overview

  • 📌 Application: GIS (Gas-Insulated Switchgear) hardened epoxy resin surface grinding & cleaning
  • 📈 Cycle Time: 5-10 pieces per hour (Front & Back)
  • 🎯 Vision Guided | Force Adaptive | Result Verified

    • The Automatic Grinding Machine for GIS Insulated Components is engineered specifically for the surface finishing of hardened epoxy resin adhered to metal parts. These components, typically used in gas-insulated electrical equipment, present unique challenges due to the material’s extreme hardness, smoothness, and resistance to mechanical stress.
    To address these challenges, Openex integrates a high-resolution 2D + 3D machine vision system and an intelligent robotic grinding arm with closed-loop force control. The result is a robust, automated system that ensures accuracy, repeatability, and tool longevity — even with the most unforgiving materials.

    Key Features

  • Guides the robotic arm with sub-millimeter accuracy

  • Industrial-grade robotic arm executes smooth, consistent grinding paths
