What is an AOI Machine?

Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) is a non-contact visual inspection method that uses industrial cameras, intelligent lighting, and machine vision software to detect defects, dimensional deviations, or surface anomalies on parts or assemblies.

Unlike traditional inspection methods, AOI allows for:

  • 100% inline inspection instead of random sampling.
  • High-speed processing without interrupting production.
  • AI-enabled detection of unexpected or difficult-to-define defects.

AOI Machine Applications

Openex AOI systems are tailored for a wide range of products and industries, including:

  • Fasteners & Mechanical Parts
    Inspection of head diameter, length, thread integrity, and surface defects; Used for bolts, screws, rivets, studs, washers, etc.
  • Automotive Components
    Detect dimensional errors, missing clips, burrs, or surface scratches on connectors, bushings, or plastic fasteners.
  • Bearings & Rotating Elements
    AOI ensures consistent geometry, smoothness, and eliminates risk of surface pitting or cracking.
  • Powder Metallurgy & Sintered Parts
    Identify porosity, cracks, or shape deviation in compacted and sintered metal components.
  • Lithium Battery Cells & Packs
    Inspect weld seams, battery terminals, casing integrity, and alignment before final assembly.
  • Rubber & Plastic Parts
    Detect deformation, color variations, sealing defects, and foreign particles.
  • Semiconductors & Electronics
    AOI for PCB inspection, connector pin alignment, solder joint verification, and OCR/OCV.
