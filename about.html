About Openex

Unparalleled expertise and experience in vision technology for automated quality control.

Who We Are

Founded at 2011

Openex Automation is a global provider of intelligent visual inspection systems and industrial automation solutions. Since our founding, we have been committed to transforming manufacturing quality control through the power of artificial intelligence, machine vision, and integrated automation.

Our mission is to empower manufacturers to achieve 100% inspection accuracy, maximize productivity, and reduce reliance on manual processes — all while improving traceability and consistency in production.

Our machine vision systems are some of the most innovative and advanced on the market today. They are used all over the world in automated production processes for quality control, inspection, guidance, identification, measurement, tracking and counting in many diverse industries.

What We Do ?

At Openex, we specialize in delivering advanced AI-powered visual inspection systems, intelligent automation solutions, and IoT-enabled smart manufacturing technologies for large-scale industrial applications. Our offerings include high-speed optical inspection equipment, AI vision software and hardware, custom non-standard automation systems, AR-based remote support platforms, and fully integrated hygiene product production lines. We empower manufacturers across automotive, electronics, medical, energy, and fastener industries to achieve zero-defect quality, enhanced productivity, and real-time operational visibility.

Our Expertise

With a team of over 60 industry professionals, including more than 10 AI experts from CSIST, Openex is uniquely positioned to design, develop, and integrate advanced machine vision systems. Our in-house capabilities span mechanical, electrical, software, and control engineering.

Our software and hardware are developed to support fast deployment, high performance, and full local execution — no cloud dependency required.

Certifications & Standards

We adhere to the highest industry standards and good automated manufacturing practices (GAMP). Our products and systems are ISO-certified and CE-marked, ensuring safety, reliability, and global compliance.
