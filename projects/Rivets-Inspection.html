Overview

The Openex Rivet Inspection Machine is a state-of-the-art automatic vision system designed to perform non-contact dimensional inspection of rivets at high speed and accuracy. Powered by advanced machine vision and AI technology, the system enables manufacturers to ensure 100% quality control of rivets, replacing manual sampling with full inspection and traceable data.

It can accurately measure multiple parameters including total length, head thickness, and various outer diameters. The flexible design allows compatibility with multiple rivet types and specifications, making it an ideal solution for high-volume fastener production lines in automotive, aerospace, and industrial manufacturing.

Key Features

High-Speed Measurement

Capable of measuring up to 300 rivets per minute with stable accuracy, ideal for high-volume production environments.

Multi-Size Compatibility

One system supports inspection of nine different rivet specifications without requiring complex hardware changeovers.

Simple & Intuitive Operation

Friendly HMI design and easy mechanical adjustment reduce setup time and training requirements for operators.
