Flange Nuts Inspection Machine — Precision Meets Intelligence

    Detect defects. Improve quality. Boost efficiency.

This AI-powered optical sorting system is engineered for the automatic inspection of M4-M20 flange nuts, including zinc-plated flange nuts.
This system eliminates human error, achieves 100% quality control, and overcomes the long-standing industry challenge of side crack detection using deep learning vision technology.

Specifications

Inspection Range: M4 - M20 Flange Nuts

Inspection Speed: up to 800 pcs/min

Camera Configuration: Multi-angle HD industrial cameras

Lighting System: Adjustable LED + Backlight + Side Light

Dimensional Measurement: Overall Height, Thread Minor Diameter, Width Across Flats (A/F), Flange Outer Diameter

Defect Detection: Burrs & Surface Scratches, Flange Side Cracks, Bottom Cracks & Flange Face Damage, Ear Presence & Deformation, Nylon Ring Presence & Deformation, Thread Presence / Absence, Flange Surface 4-Point Presence Check.

Overview

Traditional rule-based inspection systems struggle with complex reflective surfaces and microscopic cracks.

Openex's proprietary AI Deep Learning Algorithm accurately detects minute side cracks and subtle deformations even on shiny or plated flange nuts, significantly reducing false negatives and false positives.

Key Benefits:
  • Detects defects invisible to conventional vision systems
  • Learns and adapts to variations in materials and finishes
  • Operates at high speed — ideal for large-scale production lines
  • Works reliably for zinc-plated, stainless steel, and coated nuts

Intelligent Custom Features

To enhance productivity and traceability, the Openex flange nut inspection machine includes several smart extensions beyond standard inspection: Integrated Barcode Scanning

  • Operators can scan a barcode to instantly load the relevant inspection recipe and detailed parameters.
  • Simplifies setup, ensures consistency, and reduces human error.
Defective Parts Counting & Traceability
  • The system precisely counts rejected parts in real time, supporting digital quality traceability.
Good Parts Counting & Packaging Function
  • Automatically records and groups qualified products for packaging and downstream processing.
