Overview

The Inspection Machine is an intelligent AI-driven system designed for fast, accurate, and fully automated quality inspection of four-prong nuts. Capable of inspecting every piece at high speed, it replaces manual inspection and ensures zero-defect production for large-scale manufacturing.

How It Works
  • Nuts are automatically fed via a precision vibratory bowl.
  • The rotating inspection disk presents each nut to multiple industrial cameras.
  • AI vision software analyzes dimensional and surface features in milliseconds.
  • OK/NG parts are separated by pneumatic ejectors into respective bins.

This process ensures consistent, high-speed, non-contact inspection suitable for continuous production environments.

Machine Features

1, High Speed up to 200-300 pcs/min

Designed for industrial-scale production, this system delivers ultra-fast inspection performance while maintaining precision and stability.

2, Wide Compatibility

Supports various four-prong nut models and lengths with quick product changeover. Perfect for multi-spec production lines.

3, User-Friendly Operation

Simple interface, reliable operation, and minimal maintenance. Each machine can be custom-built to your production needs.

4, AI-Enhanced Accuracy

Integrated deep-learning vision software identifies complex or subtle surface defects beyond conventional inspection methods.

Every inspected part can be traced and recorded to ensure complete quality documentation.
