Overview

Our Stainless Steel Valve Core Inspection Machine is an AI-powered, non-contact vision inspection system designed specifically for detecting dimensional accuracy and hole defects in stainless steel valve cores.

It combines precision measurement, intelligent defect identification, and an automatic anti-fall feeding system to ensure stable and efficient inspection.

This system is widely used in automotive, pneumatic systems, machinery manufacturing, and precision metal parts industries, helping manufacturers replace manual sampling with 100% automated, traceable inspections.

Key Features

Customized Anti-Tipping Feeding

A specially developed loading and feeding system addresses the major difficulty of handling easily-toppled stainless steel valve cores, ensuring stable, high-speed, and continuous operation without jams.

High-Resolution Vision System

Employs industrial-grade cameras and optics for detailed inspection of minute dimensional variations and internal hole conditions.

Integrated Multi-Point Gauging

Simultaneous measurement of multiple critical dimensions (Length, OD, and Hole Height) against tight tolerances.

Comprehensive Hole Integrity Check

Advanced algorithms detect and classify all critical through-hole defects, including partial, multiple, or absent holes.

Non-Contact Measurement

Ensures no damage or wear to the precision-machined stainless steel surface during the inspection process.

User-Friendly HMI

Intuitive interface for easy setup, calibration, and real-time monitoring of inspection data and statistics.
