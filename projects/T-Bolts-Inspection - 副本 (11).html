Overview

Screws are among the most widely used fasteners across industries such as automotive, aerospace, electronics, and construction. As production volumes increase and quality standards rise, manual inspection is no longer sufficient to guarantee zero-defect screws.

Openex Screws Inspection Machine provides manufacturers with a reliable, scalable solution to automate inspection, maximize throughput, and minimize costly defects. Using high-resolution industrial cameras, AI-powered vision algorithms, and pneumatic sorting ejectors, the system ensures every screw is inspected thoroughly before packaging.

Inspection Capabilities

Our screws inspection machines are designed to detect dimensional features as well as critical surface defects: Dimensional Measurements

  • - Head diameter, height, and shape accuracy
  • - Shank diameter and length
  • - Thread length, pitch, and completeness
  • - Overall length and straightness
Surface Defects Detection
  • - Cracks, notches, and burrs
  • - Scratches, dents, and deformation
  • - Thread damage, incomplete rolling, or flattening
  • - Missing slots, incorrect recess shapes (Phillips, Torx, Hex)
Appearance & Identification
  • - Color recognition for coated screws
  • - Marking and symbol verification
  • - Mixed-part detection (rejecting wrong-type screws)
