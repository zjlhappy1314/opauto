Specifications

Inspection Speed: Up to 500 pcs/min

Bolt Range: M8 bolts, 16-60 mm length

Detection Accuracy: ±0.01 mm (dimension) / ±0.005 mm (defect localization)

Technology: AI-based Deep Learning + Traditional Vision

Dimensional Measurements: Material mix-up detection, Perpendicularity, Hex head across flats & across corners, Thread pitch and depth, Overall length and shank dimensions.

Defect Detection: Missing or incorrect head markings, Thread damage and chipping, Thread contamination or liquid residue, Missing or incomplete threads, Slot presence and shape accuracy, Hole presence verification, Foreign objects inside holes, Eccentricity between dual symmetrical holes, Cross-threading or angled threads, Incorrect thread count

Overview

Perforated and slotted bolts present unique inspection challenges due to their non-standard shapes, multiple holes, and slotted head designs. Manual inspection is often inconsistent and time-consuming, especially when inspecting both dimensional accuracy and surface integrity.

This Perforated and Slotted Bolts Inspection Machine solves this problem with a fully automated, non-contact optical inspection system driven by AI. It performs both dimensional measurement and defect detection simultaneously, ensuring no defective bolts escape your production line.

Equipped with high-resolution cameras, industrial-grade lighting, and AI-based image recognition algorithms, it provides unmatched precision and repeatability, even in high-speed operations.

Key Features

  • Ultra-High Speed: Handles up to 500 bolts per minute, ideal for continuous mass production.
  • AI-Powered Detection: Detects micro-level thread damage, residual oil, or liquid contamination that traditional systems might miss.
  • Comprehensive Inspection Coverage: Full 360° inspection using multiple synchronized industrial cameras and precision optics.
  • Flexible Compatibility: Supports M8 bolts from 16 mm to 60 mm, including slotted, perforated, and custom-shaped heads.
  • Intuitive User Interface: Simple touchscreen operation, auto-learning algorithms, and quick recipe setup for different bolt types.
  • Data Traceability: Automatic defect image recording, report generation, and quality data export for production analysis.

Benefits

  • 100% Inline Inspection — Eliminate sampling — inspect every part for complete process confidence.
  • Higher Productivity — Speed and precision combined to reduce rework, manual labor, and downtime.
  • Enhanced Quality Assurance — Detect the smallest defects and ensure zero-defect shipments.
  • Improved ROI — Quick setup, minimal maintenance, and fast defect elimination pay back quickly.
  • Adaptable to Future Production — AI models can be retrained for new bolt types or design variants without hardware change.
