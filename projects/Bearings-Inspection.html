Overview

Our AI inspection system can identify dozens of potential defects that compromise bearing quality and reliability, including:

  • Collapsed or dented dust covers
  • Scratched or damaged cages (retainers)
  • Excessive chamfer, rust, or machining marks on outer rings
  • Rust or waste material on outer ring end faces
  • Missing middle rollers
  • Inner bore irregularities or surface defects
  • Vibration marks and rust on inner ring end faces
  • Missing superfinishing or incomplete polishing
  • Deformation, discoloration, and other microscopic flaws
Each inspected bearing is automatically classified as OK/NG, with results stored digitally along with high-resolution images for analysis and reporting.
