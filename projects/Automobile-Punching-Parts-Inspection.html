Overview

The Openex Automobile Punching Parts Inspection Machine is designed for comprehensive inspection of automotive stamping components.

It ensures precise dimensional measurement and accurate defect detection to maintain the highest quality standards in mass production environments.

Our system replaces manual or sampling inspections with fully automated, traceable quality control powered by AI vision technology.
