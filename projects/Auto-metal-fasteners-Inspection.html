Overview

Automotive fasteners play a critical role in safety and performance. The Openex Auto Metal Fasteners Inspection Machine is designed to deliver reliable, high-speed dimensional and defect detection for a wide range of automotive clips and open-type metal fasteners. Powered by AI vision technology, our system ensures every piece meets stringent quality standards — eliminating the risks of manual sampling inspection.

The Auto Metal Fasteners Inspection Machine is widely used in automotive manufacturing for clips, clamps, retainers, spring fasteners, and other open-type metal parts. It is suitable for large-scale, high-speed production lines where precision and defect-free output are critical.

Key Features

  1. High-Speed Performance: Inspection speed up to 600 pcs/min for mass production environments.
  2. Stable Feeding: Equipped with a replenishment hopper and high-speed centrifugal feeder for reliable handling, even for open-type parts.
  3. Low Noise Operation: Noise-reduction design ensures smooth operation without jamming, tangling, or part damage.
  4. Excellent Compatibility: Supports inspection of various sizes and shapes of automotive fasteners and clips.
