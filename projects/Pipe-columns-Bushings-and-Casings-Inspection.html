Overview

The Pipe, Columns, Bushings & Casings Inspection Machine is an intelligent, high-speed, non-contact inspection solution designed for tubular and cylindrical components. It integrates multiple high-resolution industrial cameras, AI deep learning algorithms, and an internal endoscopic vision module to ensure all dimensions and surfaces — including inner walls — are precisely inspected.

This system allows manufacturers to move beyond manual inspection and sampling checks to achieve 100% automated inspection and traceable quality control. Whether you produce metal bushings, precision casings, or machined pipe columns, Openex’s system guarantees reliability, repeatability, and real-time defect detection in continuous production environments.

Key Features

1. High-Speed Detection

Process up to 300 components per minute, ensuring maximum throughput for mass production.

2. High Compatibility

One system supports four different part types, reducing setup time and cost.

3. AI Deep Learning Algorithms

Automatically identify complex and microscopic defects on top and bottom surfaces, threads, and edges — even under challenging lighting or reflection conditions.

4. Endoscopic Inner Wall Inspection

Specialized camera lens for internal surface defect detection, ensuring full 360° inspection coverage inside the component.

5. Modular and Expandable Design

Adaptable to new product types or integrated into existing production lines with ease.

6. Data Traceability & Reporting

Real-time image recording, defect classification, and production reporting for quality traceability.
