Overview

The Openex Bearing Races Inspection Machine is an advanced visual inspection solution developed for detecting surface and dimensional defects on bearing outer rings. Using AI-based machine vision, telecentric imaging optics, and a customized non-contact inspection platform, it guarantees defect-free bearings for high-speed production environments.

Inspection Object: Bearing Outer Rings
Inspection Range: Outer Diameter Ø19 - 62 mm
Inspection Type: Automatic Optical & AI Vision Detection

Key Features

  • 1. Wide Compatibility
    Supports various bearing outer rings with diameters between 19 mm and 62 mm, making it adaptable to diverse production lines and models.
  • 2. Telecentric Lens Imaging
    Equipped with high-precision telecentric optics, the system eliminates perspective distortion and captures ultra-clear surface details — ensuring even microscopic flaws are detected.
  • 3. Custom Non-Contact Platform
    A tailor-made inspection stage prevents any scratching or mechanical damage to the finely machined bearing surfaces during inspection.
  • 4. Stable and Reliable Operation
    Built with industrial-grade components and intuitive operation software, the system provides continuous, consistent, and user-friendly performance under production conditions.
  • 5. AI-Assisted Accuracy
    The integrated AI deep learning model automatically adapts to new defect variations, improving accuracy over time without extensive manual parameter tuning.
