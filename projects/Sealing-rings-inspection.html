    360° Visual Inspection | AI Defect Detection | Zero Missed Inspection

High-speed AI-powered visual inspection system designed for sanitary valve sealing rings. Detects micro surface defects such as burrs, cracks, dead rubber, stains, bubbles and ensures consistent product quality — fully automated, contactless, and reliable.

Specifications

Inspection Objects: Sealing rings

Inspection Speed: 600-1000 pieces/min

Feeding Method: Special-coated vibration feeding bowl to avoid material jamming

Inspection Capabilities: Inner ring burrs / small notches, Outer ring burrs, Outer diameter, Surface stains / contamination, Dead rubber, Cracks / tearing, Outer rib micro notches, Bubbles / blistering

Vision System: Multi-camera + bright-field + dark-field imaging

AI Algorithm: Deep learning-based defect detection

Overview

Sealing rings in sanitary valves must ensure perfect sealing performance with no surface damage or deformation. Manual inspection is time-consuming, inconsistent, and prone to human error.

The Sealing-Ring Inspection Machine provides a fully automated, high-precision inspection solution using AI deep learning and industrial vision. Components are automatically oriented through an anti-sticking vibration bowl, captured using dual bright/dark-field imaging, analyzed in milliseconds, and classified into OK/NG outputs.

This system is ideal for manufacturers of sanitary valves, faucets, plumbing fittings, or silicone/rubber sealing components.

Features

Advanced Part Handling

Vibratory bowl feeder with a special non-stick coating treatment, effectively preventing jamming and material stagnation for smooth, uninterrupted operation.

Static Control & Cleanliness

Integrated ionizing fan neutralizes static electricity, preventing dust attraction and part misalignment, ensuring a clean and interference-free inspection process.

High-Speed Precision Imaging

Patented bright and dark field "flying" shot detection technology captures high-resolution images on-the-fly, drastically reducing cycle times and minimizing the chance of missed inspections.

Benefits

Unmatched Quality Assurance

Achieve near-perfect defect detection rates, ensuring every sealing ring that leaves your line meets the highest quality standards.

Maximized Production Uptime

Our non-stick feeders and robust design minimize downtime caused by jams or maintenance, maximizing your overall equipment effectiveness (OEE).

Significant Cost Reduction

Reduce labor costs associated with manual inspection, minimize scrap and rework, and prevent costly warranty claims from defective parts.
