Overview

With the continuous growth of the consumer electronics industry and rising quality requirements, manufacturers face increasing pressure to ensure flawless magnet components. Traditional manual inspection of arc magnets is inefficient, labor-intensive, and error-prone. Openex provides a breakthrough solution with its ARCS Magnet Inspection Machine, replacing manual checks with fully automated, AI-driven quality control.

This system significantly reduces production costs, boosts output, and ensures high-quality standards. By adopting automated visual inspection, enterprises can keep pace with market demands and strengthen competitiveness.

Inspection Process

The Openex ARCS Magnet Inspection Machine adopts a multi-station inspection approach:

  1. Station 1: Fragment and chip detection
  2. Stations 2-7: Edge peeling detection
  3. Stations 8-11: Side crack inspection
  4. Stations 12-15: Width and chamfer surface inspection
  5. Stations 16-19: Uneven outer arc surface inspection

All defective parts are automatically rejected, ensuring only high-quality products move forward in the production line.
