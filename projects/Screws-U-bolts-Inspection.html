Specifications

Inspection Objects: Screws, U-Bolts, and similar threaded fasteners

Overview

In the critical world of fasteners, every micron matters. Our Screws & U-Bolts Inspection System delivers 100% automated, non-contact dimensional analysis, eliminating human error and ensuring every component that leaves your line is built to spec. Achieve zero-defect manufacturing and protect your brand reputation.

Built with a high-speed vision system and AI-based defect recognition algorithms, this equipment can accurately detect dimensional deviations and surface imperfections, ensuring consistent quality across every unit produced.

The system integrates seamlessly into your production line, offering real-time monitoring, defect classification, and automatic sorting of OK/NG products.

Features & Benefits

Blazing Inspection Speed

Feature: Capable of inspecting up to 60 parts per minute (3,600 per hour).
Benefit: Keeps pace with high-volume production lines without creating a bottleneck, maximizing your output and ROI.

Superior Flexibility

Feature: Highly compatible with parts up to 100mm in width, handling various screw and U-bolt designs.
Benefit: One system can inspect your entire family of similar fasteners, reducing capital expenditure and floor space requirements.

High-Precision Detection

Feature: Advanced vision system capable of detecting defects as small as 0.2mm.
Benefit: Catches minute imperfections in threads and geometry that could lead to critical failures, ensuring product reliability and safety.

Lightning-Fast Changeover

Feature: Integrated feeding ramp and a simple, stable structure allowing for model changeover in just 10 minutes.
Benefit: Drastically reduces downtime when switching product lines, enabling efficient small-batch production and enhancing overall equipment effectiveness (OEE).
