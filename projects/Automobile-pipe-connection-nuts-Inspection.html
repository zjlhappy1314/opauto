Overview

The **Washers Inspection Machine** is an automatic visual inspection machine with a rotary table for the dimensional and surface inspection of washers and generic items made of rubber, plastic, PTFE, and other materials. The inspection machines have many operational characteristics in common and offer great value for money. Artificial Intelligence software can be added upon a customer’s request and can be installed remotely at any time.

The feed system loads the parts onto a conveyor belt, then a motorized diverter transfers them onto a rotary glass table. From this point on, every single part is inspected for defects and dimensional accuracy by a series of dedicated vision inspection stations.

Inspection Capabilities

Our advanced optical inspection systems are designed to ensure 100% quality control of washers, guaranteeing dimensional accuracy and surface integrity before they reach your customers. Using high-speed cameras, precision lenses, and AI-driven image processing, our equipment detects even the smallest defects and deviations.

Key Inspection Parameters for Washers

  • Dimensional Measurement: Outer Diameter (OD), Inner Diameter (ID), Thickness, Flatness / Warpage, etc.
  • Surface Defects: Identification of scratches, cracks, dents, corrosion, or contamination.
  • Edge Defects: Detection of burrs, chips, or incomplete cuts on inner and outer edges.
  • Coating & Color: Verifies plating quality, uniformity of coating, and correct washer color.
  • Material Mix-up Prevention: Differentiates washers by material type, finish, and size.
