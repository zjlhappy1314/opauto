Overview

As electric mobility accelerates, ensuring the quality and reliability of battery accessories is critical. Openex offers a dedicated AI vision inspection system for knurled copper nuts with internal threads, used in battery module assemblies for new energy vehicles.

The system performs 100% inline inspection to guarantee dimensional accuracy and surface perfection — eliminating human error and ensuring zero-defect production.

Equipment Highlights

  • Multi-Specification Compatibility: Supports inspection of seven different nut sizes within one system.
  • High Inspection Speed: Capable of 300+ pieces per minute, ideal for mass production environments.
  • AI-Powered Defect Recognition: Integrated deep-learning algorithms adapt to subtle variations and automatically classify defects.
  • Precision Industrial Vision Hardware: High-resolution cameras, optimized lighting, and real-time image processing ensure reliable detection.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Easy parameter adjustment, auto-report generation, and visual display of OK/NG results.
  • Full Automation Ready: Can be integrated with robotic loading, sorting, and packaging systems.

System Benefits

  • 100% Non-Contact Inspection: Avoids surface damage and ensures consistent accuracy.
  • Enhanced Traceability: All inspection data stored for quality tracking and analysis.
  • Reduced Labor Cost: Fully automated inspection replaces manual sampling.
  • Improved Product Quality: Detects every defect before assembly, reducing rework and recall risk.
  • Smart Integration: Easily interfaces with MES or ERP systems for smart factory management.
