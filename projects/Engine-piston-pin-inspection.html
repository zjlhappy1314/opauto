Engine Piston Pin Inspection Machine

    High-Speed AI Vision System for Dimensional & Surface Defect Detection

Openex's Engine Piston Pin Inspection Machine provides a comprehensive automated solution for the precise inspection of piston pins used in automotive engines. Equipped with advanced industrial vision cameras and AI deep-learning algorithms, the system ensures every piston pin meets strict dimensional and surface quality standards — at production speeds impossible for manual inspection.

Specifications

Inspection Object: Engine Piston Pins

Applicable Range: Outer Diameter: 17-22 mm; Length: 38-58 mm.

Inspection Speed: 200-1200 pieces/min

Detection Accuracy: ±0.05 mm

Defect Types: Dents, pits, missing material, scratches, edge chipping, adhesion

Algorithm: Deep Learning AI + Traditional Vision Fusion

Output: Real-time data, defect images, pass/fail report

Overview

Openex Automation is a global provider of AI vision inspection and industrial automation systems. Our inspection machines are deployed in diverse sectors including automotive, bearings, fasteners, lithium batteries, and precision engineering.

This optical inspection system accommodates multiple piston pin types without tooling replacement, supporting fast model changeovers and continuous inline operation.

Benefits
  • 100% automated inline inspection - eliminate human error
  • Improved traceability - digital defect recording and quality reports
  • Faster production speed - non-stop inspection and sorting
  • Reduced costs - no sampling needed; every part is inspected
  • Higher customer satisfaction - consistent, verified product quality

Key Features & Advantages

High Compatibility

Handles multiple piston pin models within a wide size range, minimizing changeover downtime and improving production efficiency.

High Precision

Dimensional accuracy up to ±0.05 mm ensures compliance with the most demanding automotive standards.

AI Defect Recognition

Equipped with Openex's proprietary deep-learning algorithm, capable of detecting micro surface defects that traditional vision systems might miss.

Real-Time Inspection & Sorting

Each piston pin is analyzed in real-time, and defective products are automatically separated via pneumatic ejectors for zero-defect shipment assurance.

User-Friendly Interface

An intuitive interface provides real-time statistics, defect images, and quality reports, helping engineers monitor production and maintain traceability.
