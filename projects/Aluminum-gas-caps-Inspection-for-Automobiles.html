Specifications

Machine Name: Automotive Aluminum Gas Caps Inspection Machine

Inspection Object: Automotive Aluminum Gas Caps

Detectable Range: 360°

Detection Speed: 200-1200 pieces/min

Accuracy: Up to 0.01mm or higher, with excellent repeatability

Inspection Items:Total height, step outer diameters, step heights, and inner hole diameters

Optional Configuration: Top View, Side View, Bottom View, Top View (According to The Inspection Items Increase or Decrease Camera Station)

Inspection Overview

Automotive aluminum gas caps are critical safety and functional components, requiring precise dimensional accuracy to ensure leak-proof performance and seamless integration into the fuel system. Openex provides an AI-enhanced non-contact inspection system that guarantees superior measurement accuracy and repeatability.
