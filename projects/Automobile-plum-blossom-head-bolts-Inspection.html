Overview

Openex's AI-powered vision inspection machine is specially designed for automobile plum-blossom head bolts.

It performs non-contact, fully automated inspection of dimensions and defects at speeds of up to 500 pieces per minute.

With dual AI 360 inspection stations, the system ensures 360° coverage of each bolt, eliminating blind spots and guaranteeing superior quality control for automotive fastener manufacturing.

Machine Features

  • Ultra-High Inspection Speed: up to 500 bolts per minute, ideal for large-scale automotive production.
  • Dual AI360 Inspection Stations: deliver complete 360° thread inspection with zero blind zones.
  • Model Compatibility: supports M5 and M6 plum-blossom head bolts without complex mechanical changeover.
  • Precision Optical System: industrial-grade cameras and telecentric lenses ensure consistent accuracy.
  • Real-Time Data & Analysis: automatic classification of OK/NG parts and real-time monitoring via intuitive HMI.
  • Integration Ready: easily connects to automatic feeding, sorting, and packaging systems.

System Advantages

  • 100% Automated Inspection - eliminates the need for manual sampling.
  • AI-Based Defect Recognition - trained neural networks detect micro-level defects.
  • Non-Contact Measurement - ensures no damage to bolt surfaces.
  • Fast Changeover - adaptable for different bolt types and specifications.
  • Enhanced Traceability - digital storage of images and measurement data for each bolt.
  • High ROI - reduces labor cost and improves product consistency.
