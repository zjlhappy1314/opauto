Overview

In the automotive industry, brake fluid circuit connectors are safety-critical components that require 100% inspection. Openex's Automobile Brake Fluid Circuit Connectors Inspection System integrates advanced cameras, tailored lighting, and intelligent software to detect defects that manual inspection often misses. Designed for high-volume production, this system ensures precision, consistency, and traceability.

This inspection system is tailored for the automotive manufacturing industry, specifically for brake fluid circuit connectors. It can be integrated into high-speed production lines for Tier-1 and Tier-2 automotive suppliers, ensuring compliance with global automotive safety standards.

Equipment Features

  1. Advanced Imaging: Equipped with color industrial cameras and custom-designed lighting modules to capture hard-to-detect surface defects.
  2. Customized Feeding System: Engineered to handle the unique shape of brake connectors, preventing part misalignment and feeding instability.
  3. High-Speed Performance: Inspection speed up to 150 pcs/min, ensuring efficiency for large-scale automotive production lines.

Benefits

  • 100% inline inspection ensuring zero-defect delivery
  • Improved safety and compliance for automotive brake systems
  • Reduced reliance on manual sampling and operator fatigue
  • Data traceability and defect categorization for quality management
