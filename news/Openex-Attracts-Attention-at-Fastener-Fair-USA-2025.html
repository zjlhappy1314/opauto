Fastener Fair USA 2025: A Key Industry Event

Fastener Fair USA is one of the most influential exhibitions in North America dedicated to the fastener industry. The 2025 edition, held at the Music City Center in Nashville, brought together a wide range of buyers from the automotive industry as well as other manufacturing sectors. The event attracted strong participation from companies with growing demand for fasteners, fastener equipment, and advanced inspection technologies.


Openex Showcases AI-Powered Optical Inspection Equipment

As a leading innovator in AI vision inspection systems, Openex (Kaiyi Intelligent) presented its latest optical inspection machine at the show. Our solutions are designed primarily for:

  • Surface defect detection of industrial components
  • Dimensional measurement of fasteners and small parts



Built for manufacturers with large-scale production and high-quality requirements, Openex inspection machines deliver:

  • Full-scale 100% product inspection
  • High-speed and high-accuracy measurements
  • AI-powered algorithms for complex defect detection
  • User-friendly software interface for easy operation

These advantages made our booth a strong highlight for visitors seeking automated inspection solutions to improve efficiency, reduce costs, and ensure quality compliance.


Why Openex Solutions Stand Out

Many manufacturers face persistent challenges in fastener defect inspection and automated measurement. Traditional manual inspection is time-consuming, inconsistent, and not scalable for mass production..

Openex addresses these challenges with:

  • AI-based vision algorithms capable of detecting subtle cracks, scratches, and dimensional inconsistencies.
  • Flexible platform design that adapts to different fastener types and other small industrial parts.
  • High-speed optical inspection systems that combine precision with productivity, ensuring reliable results in real time.

By integrating advanced AI vision inspection with robust hardware, Openex provides solutions that align perfectly with the needs of fastener producers, automotive suppliers, and other precision manufacturing sectors.

Strong Market Interest at Fastener Fair USA

Our cutting-edge systems generated strong interest among fastener manufacturers, automotive OEMs, and component suppliers at the exhibition. Visitors were particularly impressed by the ability of Openex equipment to:

  • Provide 100% inline defect detection
  • Perform accurate dimensional measurement without slowing production
  • Solve previously unsolved inspection challenges with AI-driven solutions

The enthusiastic response highlights the growing demand in North America for automated fastener inspection systems and AI-powered quality control solutions.
