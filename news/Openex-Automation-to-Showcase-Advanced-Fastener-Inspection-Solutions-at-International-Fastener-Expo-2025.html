Openex Automation to Showcase Advanced Fastener Inspection Solutions at International Fastener Expo 2025

September 2025 - Openex Automation is pleased to announce that we will be exhibiting at the International Fastener Expo (IFE) 2025, taking place on September 16-17, 2025, at Mandalay Bay, Las Vegas. You can find us at Booth #2326, where we will showcase our latest intelligent fastener inspection systems, automation solutions, and AI-powered vision technologies.


The International Fastener Expo is North America's largest gathering of fastener professionals, bringing together manufacturers, distributors, suppliers, and industry experts from around the world. Each year, the event highlights innovations in fastener production, assembly technology, and quality assurance.

At this year's expo, Openex Automation will feature:

  • High-Speed Optical Inspection Machines - designed for bolts, screws, washers, nuts, rivets, and other critical fasteners.Capture an image of an object
  • AI-Driven Vision Systems - delivering enhanced accuracy in detecting dimensional variances, surface defects, and assembly errors.
  • Customized Automation Solutions - integrating robotics, motion control, and smart sorting for high-volume production lines.
  • Industrial IoT Integration - enabling real-time data monitoring and traceability for advanced manufacturing environments.

"We're excited to connect with fastener manufacturers, distributors, and OEMs at IFE 2025," said Bell Peng, CEO. "Our mission is to help customers achieve higher productivity, zero-defect quality control, and smarter manufacturing with our advanced inspection and automation technologies."

Openex Automation's solutions are trusted across industries including automotive, aerospace, electronics, medical devices, and renewable energy. By combining optical inspection, machine vision, and automation, we deliver reliable systems that ensure 100% quality control for fasteners and industrial components.

We invite all attendees to visit us at Booth #2326 to see live demonstrations, explore our inspection capabilities, and discuss customized solutions for your manufacturing needs.

This makes vision systems especially valuable in industries where precision is non-negotiable: automotive, electronics, food and beverage, pharmaceuticals, and medical devices.

For more information about the event, please visit the International Fastener Expo 2025 website: https://fastenerexpo.com
