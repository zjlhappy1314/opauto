CONTROL 2025: The Global Stage for Quality Control

The CONTROL Exhibition in Stuttgart is recognized as the world's largest and most influential trade fair for quality assurance and metrology. Every year, it brings together leading global brands, advanced technologies, and forward-looking innovations in the field of quality control, testing, and instrumentation.

At CONTROL 2025, industry giants such as Zeiss, Renishaw, and Hexagon will present their latest dimensional measurement solutions. Domestic leaders including Zhongtuo Instruments, Nameiteke, and Tianzhun Technology will also be on display.

Amid this strong lineup, Openex (Kaiyi Intelligent) will stand out as one of the few exhibitors focusing not only on dimensional measurement, but more importantly on surface defect detection for industrial components.


Openex: Specializing in Surface Defect Detection + Dimensional Measurement

Unlike most exhibitors who focus solely on dimensional metrology, Openex differentiates itself with a dual approach:

  • Surface defect inspection for industrial components
  • High-speed dimensional measurement

This combination makes Openex solutions especially valuable to manufacturers of small parts with high quality requirements and large-scale production needs.

Our inspection systems are designed for:

  • 100% full-part inspection - ensuring every component meets strict standards
  • High-speed operation - boosting productivity without sacrificing accuracy
  • User-friendly interface - simplifying operation for manufacturing teams
  • AI-powered algorithms - solving complex real-world defect detection challenges that traditional systems struggle with


Why Our Solutions Attract Global Manufacturers

Openex inspection equipment is particularly attractive to customers because it directly addresses their pain points in mass production and quality control:

  • Eliminates missed defects through AI-driven recognition
  • Reduces manual inspection costs with automated optical inspection
  • Ensures stable quality in high-volume environments
  • Enhances competitiveness for suppliers serving automotive, electronics, aerospace, and precision engineering industries

For companies where defect-free small parts are critical to safety and performance, Openex provides a commercially powerful solution that integrates speed, accuracy, and intelligence.
