Openex Automation Captures Attention at International Fastener Expo 2025 with Innovative Compact Inspection System

[Las Vegas, USA] - September 2025 - The International Fastener Expo (IFE) 2025, held on September 16-17 at Mandalay Bay, Las Vegas, concluded successfully with thousands of professionals from the global fastener industry in attendance. Openex Automation was proud to showcase its latest innovations at Booth #2326, drawing strong interest from manufacturers, distributors, and OEMs seeking advanced inspection and automation solutions.


A Highlight of the Expo: Compact Fastener Inspection Machine

As a technology company dedicated to surface defect detection and dimensional measurement of industrial components, Openex presented a compact, portable fastener optical inspection system that became one of the show's highlights.

  • The entire system fits inside a 28-inch suitcase, with a total weight of less than 50 kg.
  • Despite its small size, it delivers comprehensive inspection functions, including dimensional checks and surface defect detection.
  • Its portability and powerful capabilities impressed many visitors, particularly fastener manufacturers from North America, who described it as “small in appearance but incredibly powerful at heart.”

This creative demo machine not only demonstrated Openex's engineering capabilities but also sparked lively discussions on the future of portable inspection solutions for the fastener industry.

Advancing with AI and Industrial Large Models

Beyond hardware innovations, Openex continues to push boundaries in R&D and AI integration. By introducing industrial large-model technology into our inspection systems, we've achieved significant breakthroughs in accuracy, efficiency, and scalability:

  • Reduced defect sample requirements to nearly one-third of traditional methods.
  • Expanded detection range and enhanced stability, ensuring reliable performance in demanding production environments.
  • High-speed, high-precision full inspection, providing a cost-effective and dependable alternative to manual inspection.

These advancements make Openex's AI-driven optical inspection systems ideal for manufacturers with large-scale production and stringent quality requirements, helping them save labor, reduce costs, improve efficiency, and strengthen their reputation for quality.

Positive Reactions from the Expo Floor

During IFE 2025, our booth attracted strong attention from visitors across different segments of the fastener supply chain. Many attendees stopped to learn more about Openex's inspection solutions, sparking constructive conversations about the role of AI, automation, and industrial IoT in ensuring quality and competitiveness.

Looking Ahead

Openex Automation's presence at IFE 2025 underscored our commitment to delivering innovative, intelligent inspection solutions that meet the evolving needs of global fastener manufacturers.

We thank all visitors who stopped by our booth, and we look forward to continuing these conversations and exploring future collaborations to drive forward the next era of smart, reliable, and efficient fastener inspection.
