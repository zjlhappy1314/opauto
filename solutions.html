Our Core Solutions

Custom Vision Inspection

Our AI vision inspection systems provide automated, high-speed, non-contact inspection of fasteners, automotive parts, bearings, lithium batteries, semiconductor components, and more. Integrated deep learning algorithms detect complex and minute defects to ensure every product meets the highest quality standards.

Industrial Automation

We're a premier machine vision and inspection integrator providing superior vision system solutions for challenging applications. We are experts in Vision & Inspection driven solutions for automating and enhancing your processes whether for a machine cell, a production line or a manufacturing plant.

AR Remote Assistance

Our augmented reality (AR) remote solutions empower your teams with real-time remote troubleshooting and inspection capabilities. This innovative technology enhances operational efficiency across manufacturing, pharmaceutical, automotive, agriculture, and healthcare sectors.
