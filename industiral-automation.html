Overview

Openex provides comprehensive Industrial Automation Solutions designed to enhance efficiency, reliability, and flexibility in modern manufacturing environments. With decades of engineering expertise, we integrate cutting-edge technologies such as robotics, PLCs, HMIs, machine vision, motion control, pneumatics, hydraulics, and process automation to deliver turnkey automation systems tailored to customer needs.

Our automation solutions cover a wide range of industries including automotive, electronics, medical, pharmaceutical, plastics, textiles, energy, and more. From small-scale precision assembly to large-scale automated production lines, Openex ensures consistent quality, higher throughput, and reduced operational costs.

Automate quicker and easier

Our advanced range of defect detection vision systems are simple to set up and provide unparalleled ease of use. Openex systems are used in all areas of factory automation, from quality inspection and part identification through to robot control.

Integration with robots, actuators, pneumatics and control is straight-forward thanks to full process control integration built into the system. And, because our cameras are some of the smallest in the vision industry, they can be easily deployed in all areas of the production line and processes.

Openex can become a major contributor to help increase your productivity, improve your yield and increase overall quality.
