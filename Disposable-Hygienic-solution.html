Disposable Hygienic Production Line

Overview

Openex provides custom converting machines and converting solutions for disposable hygienic products. This includes equipment design, manufacturing, installation, debugging, training, and maintenance. Our technicians each have more than 20 years of experience in the disposable hygiene converting industry, allowing us to offer a full service range to meet strict requirements for a variety of hygienic products with a reliable performance, high efficiency and low cost.

Openex is committed to carrying out each project according to all customer requirements and custom service options. By understanding a customer’s needs, we are then able to offer the best hygienic product manufacturing solution. The manufacturing department determines the best technology and processes to ensure a favorable price without compromising production capacity or quality for our customers. We strictly control raw material and finished equipment quality and respond quickly to any after-sales questions or problems that may arise. We also keep a variety of products in stock to quickly meet replacement and maintenance requirements.

Composite Core Machine

High-speed composite absorbing core machine, using full servo drive system, can efficiently and stably produce multi-function composite core.

  • Production capacity: 400~600PPM;
  • Online visual inspection of product size and appearance defects;
  • Core flow channel online forming cutting;
  • Precise polymer addition;
  • Touch screen operating system;
  • Can be equipped with wool winder or van winder


KN95 Mask Making Machine

  • The equipment adopts Siemens servo control system, touch screen setting is easy to operate, constant tension uncoiling, less scrap, high efficiency, maintenance-free heat sealing unit, high reliability, easy maintenance, etc.


Vision Inspection

Our ultra-high-speed intelligent image detection system uses advanced high-speed image acquisition technology and AI processing algorithm to automatically learn detection parameters, and realize high-precision online detection of the size, material availability and appearance defects of various sanitary products, so as to ensure the accuracy and consistency of products.


Equipment Upgrading

We provide renovation and upgrading services for automation equipment in the product line, such as: upgrading servo drive, integrated vision inspection system, ultrasonic online edging of trouser products, flexible printing system of personalized products, etc. Whether it is the need to improve product performance or increase the production efficiency of equipment, we can provide the final solution for design, manufacturing and commissioning based on our extensive industry experience.
